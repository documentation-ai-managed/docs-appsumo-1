## Applying to Become a Seller

Start by submitting your application through the AppSumo seller portal. AppSumo reviews products for quality, uniqueness, and market fit. Expect a response within 7-10 business days.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up at `https://appsumo.com/seller` with your business email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Application" icon="upload">
    Provide product details, demo video, and pricing proposal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Product Review" icon="check-circle">
    AppSumo team evaluates your submission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Onboarding" icon="settings">
    Sign agreements and set up your seller dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Prepare a 2-minute demo video showcasing your product's key features. Highlight time savings for users.
</Callout>

## Preparing Product Listings and Pricing

Craft compelling listings with high-quality screenshots, benefit-focused copy, and competitive pricing. AppSumo specializes in lifetime deals priced from `$29` to `$199` per tier.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Deal Structure" icon="layers">
    Use tiered pricing to upsell features.

    | Tier | Price | Features |
    |------|-------|----------|
    | Basic | `$29` | Core tools |
    | Pro   | `$59` | Advanced analytics |
    | Elite | `$99` | Priority support + API access |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Listing Copy" icon="edit-3">
    Focus on benefits: "Save 10 hours/week on task automation."

    ```markdown
    ## TaskMaster Pro

    Automate your workflow and reclaim your time.

    **Key Benefits:**
    - Integrate with 50+ apps
    - No-code automations
    - Lifetime access
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Visuals" icon="image">
    Upload 5-7 screenshots and a GIF demo. Use tools like Loom for videos.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing Sales and Customer Support

Once live, monitor sales via your dashboard. Handle support through AppSumo's integrated system or your own tools. Respond to inquiries within 24 hours to maintain high ratings.

<CodeGroup tabs="API,Dashboard">
  ```javascript
  // Fetch sales data via AppSumo API
  const response = await fetch('https://api.appsumo.com/sellers/sales', {
    headers: { Authorization: `Bearer ${YOUR_API_KEY}` }
  });
  const sales = await response.json();
  console.log(sales.totalRevenue);
  ```
  ```bash
  # Dashboard metrics
  curl -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  https://api.appsumo.com/sellers/metrics
  ```
</CodeGroup>

Integrate with Zendesk or Intercom for seamless support.

## Tracking Earnings and Payouts

AppSumo pays weekly via PayPal or wire transfer after a 14-day hold. Track earnings in real-time.

<Expandable title="Payout Schedule" default-open="true">
  Payouts occur every Monday for sales from the prior week.

  - Minimum payout: `$100`
  - Fees: 5% platform fee + payment processing
  - Refunds: Handled automatically; netted from future payouts
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Earnings Report Example">
  ```json
  {
    "totalSales": 1500,
    "revenue": "$45,000",
    "payouts": [
      {"date": "2024-10-07", "amount": "$20,000"}
    ]
  }
  ```
</Expandable>

<Callout kind="success">
  Top sellers earn over `$1M` in first-year revenue. Optimize based on buyer feedback.
