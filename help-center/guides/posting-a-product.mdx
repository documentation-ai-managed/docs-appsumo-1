---
title: How to List Your Product on AppSumo
description: Quick guide to get your software listed as a partner.
---

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply as a Partner">
    Go to https://partners.appsumo.com and click "Apply Now" to submit your product details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding">
    Fill out the partnership form and submit screenshots, descriptions, and pricing info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Launch">
    AppSumo reviews applications. If approved, your product will go live on the marketplace!
  </Step>
</Steps>
