## One-Time Purchase Model Benefits

AppSumo revolutionizes software acquisition with lifetime deals. You pay once and own the software forever, dodging endless SaaS subscriptions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Deals" icon="search">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Details" icon="eye">
    Check features, vendor info, and refund policy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Purchase" icon="shopping-cart">
    Complete checkout with one-time payment via card or PayPal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Instantly" icon="download">
    Receive license keys and setup instructions immediately.
  </Step>

This model saves you thousands long-term. For example, a `{299}` deal replaces `{49}/month` plans, yielding payback in under a year.

## Customer Reviews and Ratings System

Trust fuels your decisions on AppSumo. Each deal features verified reviews from real users, with a 5-star rating system.

| Rating | Description | Typical Feedback |
|--------|-------------|------------------|
| 5 Stars | Exceptional value | "Transformed my workflow—worth every penny!" |
| 4 Stars | Strong performer | "Great features, minor learning curve." |
| 3 Stars | Functional but limited | "Does the job, but lacks advanced options." |
| 2 Stars | Below expectations | "Basic; better free alternatives exist." |
| 1 Star | Avoid | "Bugs galore; poor support." |

Reviews include screenshots and videos for authenticity. You sort by "Most Helpful" or recency.

<Expandable title="How Ratings Work" default-open="true">
  AppSumo moderates reviews post-purchase only. You earn badges for detailed feedback, boosting visibility.
</Expandable>
