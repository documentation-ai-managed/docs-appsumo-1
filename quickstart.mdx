## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and verify your email quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit AppSumo" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [https://appsumo.com](https://appsumo.com) and click **Sign Up** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and agree to the terms. Click **Create Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from AppSumo. Click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Complete Your First Purchase

Select a deal and proceed to checkout. Here's how.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a Deal" icon="shopping-cart">
    Click **Get Deal** on any featured product. Review lifetime access details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Cart" icon="plus">
    Select your tier (e.g., 1 user or team) and click **Get [Deal Name]**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Checkout" icon="credit-card">
    Enter payment details or select saved method. Apply any promo code if available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Credit Card" icon="credit-card">
    Enter card number, expiry (`MM/YY`), CVV, and billing address.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="PayPal" icon="dollar-sign">
    Click **Pay with PayPal** and log in to complete securely.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Watch for limited-time deals like SHOP SUMO DAY for extra savings.
</Callout>

## Navigate Your Dashboard

After purchase, access your orders and downloads from the dashboard.

<Expandable title="Dashboard Overview" default-open="true">
  Log in and click **My Account** > **Purchases**. Here you find:

  | Section     | Description                          |
  |-------------|--------------------------------------|
  | Orders      | View purchase history and invoices. |
  | Downloads   | Get software installers/licenses.    |
  | Vouchers    | Redeem codes for SaaS access.        |
  | Refunds     | Manage returns within policy.        |

  Track deal updates via email notifications.
</Expandable>
