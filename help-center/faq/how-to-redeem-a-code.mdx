## Overview

After you purchase a deal, you’ll receive a unique code to access the product.

## Steps

1. Go to the deal page in your AppSumo account.
2. Click "Redeem now" to view your unique code.
3. Go to the product’s redemption URL (provided in your account).
4. Enter your code and follow on-screen instructions.

<Callout kind="tip">
Redemption links and instructions vary by product—always review the deal page for details.
</Callout>
