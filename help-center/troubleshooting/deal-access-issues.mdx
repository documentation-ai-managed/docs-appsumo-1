---
title: Deal Not Showing In My Account
description: Quick steps to solve missing deal access after purchase.
---

## Symptoms
- Purchased deal, but it's missing from "Products" in your AppSumo account

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh & Check">
    Log out and log back into your AppSumo account. Refresh your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email Check">
    Look for a purchase confirmation and redemption details in your inbox (check spam folder).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    Still missing? Email support@appsumo.com with your order number for help.
  </Step>
</Steps>
